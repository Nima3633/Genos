About Genos AI

A new layer of
intelligence for work

Genos AI was founded on a simple belief: the most capable teams will be those that augment human judgment with intelligent agents. We build the infrastructure and tooling that makes deploying safe, effective AI agents accessible to every organization — from fast-growing startups to global enterprises.

Our agents are designed not to replace your team, but to amplify it — handling repetitive complexity so people can spend time on creative, strategic, and interpersonal work.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Genos AI ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, and password when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Genos AI platform, including agent activity logs, feature usage, and session data.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and operating system.
  • Communications: Any messages or feedback you send to us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, authenticate your identity, send important notices and updates, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, and analyze usage trends to improve the product.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud infrastructure and analytics partners) under strict confidentiality agreements, and as required by law or legal process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@genos.ai.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and regular security audits. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@genos.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and functionality, and optional analytics cookies to understand usage. You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
