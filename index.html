Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Genos AI ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our platform and SMS messaging services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, and password when you create an account.
  • Phone Numbers: Mobile phone numbers voluntarily provided when you opt in to receive SMS communications from Genos AI.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Genos AI platform, including agent activity logs, feature usage, and session data.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and operating system.
  • Communications: Any messages or feedback you send to us.

3. SMS Communications & Phone Number Data

We do not share or sell your personal information or phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers collected through our SMS opt-in program are used solely to deliver the specific communications you have consented to receive from Genos AI.

When you opt in to receive SMS messages from Genos AI, you agree to receive automated text messages at the number you provide. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. To re-subscribe, reply START. For help, reply HELP or contact support@genosai.com.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, authenticate your identity, send important notices and updates (including via SMS where consented), respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, and analyze usage trends to improve the product.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or phone numbers with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud infrastructure, SMS delivery, and analytics partners) strictly as needed to operate our services, under confidentiality agreements that prohibit them from using your data for any other purpose. We may also disclose data as required by applicable law or legal process.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. SMS opt-in records are retained for a minimum of 4 years to comply with telecommunications regulations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at privacy@genosai.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, strict access controls, and regular security audits. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@genosai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and functionality, and optional analytics cookies to understand usage. You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
